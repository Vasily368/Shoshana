baby loss

Experiencing theloss of a pregnancy, infant,or child brings

unimaginable pain grief and isolation.

It can feel like nobody understands your loss.

Even your partner can't really understand, because they are grieving in their own way. It likely hurts more than anything you have ever experienced. Life doesn't seem fair right now and it can be hard to imagine moving forward.

You may be going through motions at work,with your significant other and perhaps even with your other children.You want to feel understood; you want a safe space to share your grief, to express your emotions, and to heal.

Life After Baby Loss Founded by Shoshana Traum, CLC

Fcuses on helping women and their spouses with mental health issues related to reproduction including infertility, miscarriage, infant loss, birth trauma, and pregnancy and postpartum stress.

ABOUT
SHOSHANA

Life Coach - Founder of Life After Baby Loss.

When I lost my five days old daughter Lilly, I was devastated. But I knew I wanted to find healing. I needed to be whole again and live the best life I could to honor Lilly.

Over the next few years I faced unexplained infertility, a misscarriage and then a high risk pregnancy.

I started to search for the way.

Going through this journey taught me a lot. Soon, friends and family started sending others to me so I could share what I had worked so hard to learn.

After over a decade of helping women through the challenge of baby loss, I received training and certification to bring my assistance to the next level so that I could share Life After Baby Loss with a broader audience.

I wish there was no need for Life After Baby Loss.

But as long as there are women in pain

I will be here to help.

I love my work and feel fortunate that I am able to spend my days supporting other women. I am inspired by my clients' courage and their pursuit of a life well lived.

When I am not with my clients, I treasure spending time with my family. I also love being with friends, traveling, reading and staying physically active.
